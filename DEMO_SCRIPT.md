# KnewBall Demo Script

## One-Liner

KnewBall turns football takes into proof-of-ball reputation on X Layer.

## Story

Football takes disappear after full-time. KnewBall locks them before kickoff, resolves results, lets fans claim Ball IQ, and turns every prediction into a public proof receipt.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the homepage.
   - Say: "KnewBall is for fans who want proof they knew ball before kickoff."

2. Launch the matchboard.
   - Show open matches.
   - Say: "Fans can browse matches first. They only connect when they are ready to lock a call."

3. Connect wallet and show profile.
   - Say: "The fan profile is wallet-owned and signature verified."

4. Open a match and make a call.
   - Pick winner, score, over/under, both teams to score, and first team to score.
   - Say: "This is not editable after lock."

5. Lock the call.
   - Confirm wallet transaction.
   - Open explorer transaction.
   - Say: "The prediction is now stored through KnewBallCup on X Layer."

6. Open proof page.
   - Show locked timestamp and kickoff timestamp.
   - Say: "This receipt proves the call was made before kickoff."

7. Resolve the match.
   - Use admin fallback or auto-resolver.
   - Say: "Results settle through the contract, then Supabase mirrors the verified outcome for the app."

8. Claim Ball IQ.
   - Confirm wallet transaction.
   - Say: "The contract prevents double claims."

9. Show Matchday Verdict.
   - Show correct reads, Ball IQ earned, badges, and current form.
   - Say: "Good calls become public reputation. Bad calls stay public too."

10. Open profile and leaderboard.
    - Show call history, proof links, Top Fans, and Country Form.
    - Say: "KnewBall turns prediction history into fan status."

## Closing

KnewBall makes football judgment verifiable, shareable, and competitive on X Layer.
